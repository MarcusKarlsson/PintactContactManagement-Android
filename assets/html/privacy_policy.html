In particular, the law provides that companies must inform consumers about the categories of personal information that have been shared with third parties, the names and addresses of those third parties, and examples of the types of services or products marketed by those third parties. To request a copy of the information disclosure provided by Pintact pursuant to Section 1798.83 of the California Civil Code, please contact us via the email or address stated below.
